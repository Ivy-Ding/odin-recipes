One Pot Slow-Cooked Spaghetti Sauce Recipe

Description

A summary of this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4x28oz canned tomatoes
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 2 meidum sized carrots
  • 2lbs ground beef (optional, 85% lean recommended)
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablesppoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli pepper
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. optional: cook the ground beef, drain the fat out when done
  2. preparation: set the oven to 325F
  3. preparation: cut the onions and carrots to a blender-friendly size
  4. add garlic and spices to oiled pot, cook for 30-40 seconds on medium heat
  5. add 3 cans of canned tomatoes and vegetables
  6. add salt and papper to taste
  7. bring to a simmer
  8. put the pot in the oven with the lid slightly ajar
  9. cook for 3-4 hours, stir every 1/2 hours
  10. remove from oven, blend everything until smooth
  11. add remaining can of tomatoes and beef
  12. simmer for 10-15 minutes, low heat, lid on
